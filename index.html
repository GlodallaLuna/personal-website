About me.

Hello, I'm a curiosity-driven front-end developer with a background in art. The Internet is where I learnt a lot of useful skills and now I want to contribute by building things for the web. I love combining my passion for design with my technical knowledge to create functional and good looking products without sacrificing creativity.

As a developer, the qualities I strive to implement in my products include:
  • An intuitive and satisfying user experience.
  • A simple and thoughtful design.
  • Clean and maintainable code.
I'm excited to keep learning and refining my skills with the right company. Feel free to reach out and say hello.
